Delta Technological University (DTU) is a prominent public Egyptian university established by Law No. 72 of 2019 to spearhead a new practical educational track in Egypt. Situated in the heart of the Qewaisna Industrial Zone in the Menofia Governorate, the university spans 96,000 square meters. Its strategic location allows students to balance classroom knowledge with practical, hands-on training in nearby factories. It is fully affiliated with the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research.Academic ProgramsThe academic structure centers around the Faculty of Industry and Energy Technology. The university provides highly tailored, 4-year technical paths across several specializations:Mechatronics Technology: Covers advanced mechanical, electronic, and control systems.Autotronics Technology: Focuses on automotive electronics and advanced manufacturing.Information Technology: Based primarily at the university’s dedicated Sadat City branch.New and Renewable Energy: Concentrates on clean, green energy systems.Prosthetics and Orthotics: Bridges biomedical devices with technical engineering.Refrigeration and Air Conditioning: Covers complex modern thermal control systems.Degrees and StructureThe university utilizes a tiered path, merging practical on-field training with engineering theory:Professional Advanced Diploma: Awarded after completing the first 2 years.Professional Bachelor of Technology: Awarded upon completion of the full 4-year track.Postgraduate Degrees: DTU offers professional Master's and Doctoral tracks for continuous higher learning.Facilities and AdmissionInfrastructure: Features over 40 technical workshops and state-of-the-art laboratory spaces.Admissions: Students apply online through the official Egyptian government coordination system.Contact Information: You can contact them via phone at +20 48 2590372 or through email at info@dtu.edu.eg
